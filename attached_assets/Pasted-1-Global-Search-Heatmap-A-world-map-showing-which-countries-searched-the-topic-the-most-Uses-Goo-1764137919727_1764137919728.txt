1. Global Search Heatmap

A world map showing which countries searched the topic the most.
(Uses Google Trends “Interest by Region.”)

Label examples:
Top Countries
Highest Interest Country
Emerging Regions

2. Top Rising Search Queries (Breakout List)

A dynamic list showing fast-rising queries from the last 24 hours.

Example rows:
Keyword – Rising +250%
Keyword – Breakout (exploding)

This makes pages feel “Google Trends expert.”

3. Sentiment Indicator (AI)

A quick AI-generated sentiment score (0–100):

Positive mentions
Negative mentions
Neutral buzz

Example:
Sentiment Score: 72/100
Public tone is mostly positive.

4. News Pulse (AI Summary of latest news)

AI summarizes the last 3–5 news headlines related to the topic.

Example:
“Recent news mentions indicate increased coverage due to a new release/event.”

This adds freshness for SEO.

5. TikTok Viral Score

AI evaluates TikTok search volume / hashtags.

Example:
TikTok Viral Score: 83/100
Trending heavily on TikTok the past 48 hours.

6. Google Search Forecast (AI)

AI predicts next 7 days interest with a mini graph or simple text.

Example:
Forecast: +18% expected increase next week.

7. Related People / Entities (AI Graph)

A visual relation:
Amazon → Jeff Bezos → Prime → Black Friday → AWS

These “knowledge graph style” sections keep users engaged.

8. Trend Risk Score (unique feature)

Shows how unstable the topic is.

0 = very stable
100 = might crash tomorrow

Example:
Trend Risk Score: 22/100 (Very stable)

Great for investors, creators, marketers.

AI checks social trend strength:

Instagram Buzz

TikTok Buzz

Auto-tag the topic:

Category:
Technology → E-commerce → Amazon → Shopping Trends

Helps internal linking & SEO.

How hard it is to rank for the keyword (0–100).
